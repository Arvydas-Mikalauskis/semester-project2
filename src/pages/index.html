Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of our FAQs. If you have any other questions you'd like answered please feel free to contact us.

What is auction

An auction is a public sale where goods or services are sold to the highest bidder. At GlobalBid, we host an online platform where users can list items for auction and others can place bids to purchase them. It’s a dynamic marketplace where the price is determined by the community’s demand.

How it works

Our auction process is simple:
List an Item: As a registered user, you can list an item for auction with a starting bid.
Place bids: Interested buyers can place bids on items they desire.
Winning the auction: When the auction time ends, the highest bid wins the item.
Transaction: The winner pays for the item using credits, and the seller receives credits in return.

How to get credits

Credits are the currency of GlobalBid. Here’s how you can get them:
Sign-Up Bonus: Get 1,000 credits just for signing up.
Sell Items: Earn credits by listing and selling your items.

Delivery info

Once you win an auction, the seller will provide delivery options based on the item’s location and size. GlobalBid facilitates the connection between buyer and seller to ensure a smooth transaction and delivery process. For international shipping, additional fees and customs regulations may apply.
